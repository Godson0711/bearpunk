1. Introduction

Bear Punks is not just another NFT collection. It's a bold vision of utility, community, and blockchain-powered adventure. Our goal is to merge digital collectibles with real value, offering holders more than art — a gateway to experiences, rewards, and innovation.

2. What is Bear Punks?

Bear Punks is a limited collection of 1,111 uniquely illustrated digital bears. Each NFT is a hand-crafted totem of identity and utility — symbolizing strength, community, and tech-forward thinking. These NFTs live on the Ethereum blockchain and serve as a key to the entire Bear Punks ecosystem.

Our artwork is designed with care and culture. Every Bear carries a story, and as a holder, you’re not just collecting art — you’re becoming part of a movement built on transparency, long-term value, and access.
